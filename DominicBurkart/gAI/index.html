gAI (Gay Eye)

gAI is a series of data-oriented projects focused on collecting and processing information concerning LGBTQ+ populations. The active gAI projects are:

  • les dossiers gais, a service which collects and emails you information about local, state, and federal candidates for each election.
  • social media analysis of LGBTQ+ populations
  • analysis of crime and hate speech directed towards LGBTQ+ populations
  • digitization of archival LGBTQ+ materials

gAI is run by Dominic Burkart. Follow @gay_eye for updates on new findings and features. Interested in this kind of work? Get involved with gAI or see related projects.
