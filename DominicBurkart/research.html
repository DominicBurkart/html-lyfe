As an editor, I founded and curate Standard Deviation, an interdisciplinary queer zine. As a researcher, I'm interested in how humans relate to others and how we construct reality.

Current Projects

Emotionality, Sociality, and Identification with Queerness

Queerness has social and emotional dimensions. Is identification with socially-constructed, non-normative identities associated with greater emotionality and social thinking? Current stage: collection.


Moral Contagion in Queer Internet Populations

Applying contagion narratives to moral messages has been effective at analyzing the spread of ideological beliefs across large, digital social networks. How do these analyses differ in Queer populations? Current stage: collection.


Interpersonal transfer of moral emotions shapes endorsement of moral content

Led by William Brady and Julian Wills, this study analyzes Twitter behaviors of large populations and explores how moral ideas spread through large social networks. Current stage: analysis.
