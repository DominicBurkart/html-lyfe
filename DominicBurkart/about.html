I'm an intermedia artist, programmer, and psychological researcher with a particular interest in queerness, empathy, networks, and neuroscience. I love to study human existence!

I grew up in Kansas City and studied at the University of Missouri: Kansas City from the fall of 2013 to the summer of 2015. I moved to New York City in the fall of 2015 and will graduate in the Spring of 2017 with a BA in Psychology and a minor in Computer Science from New York University.
