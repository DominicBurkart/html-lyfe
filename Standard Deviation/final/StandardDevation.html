Standard Deviation was started in 2016 an interdisciplinary journal for queer thought to be developed and explored. The objective of Standard Deviation is to emphasize queer narratives, which are a powerful cultural technology of criticism and innovation.

Standard Deviation has no institutional affiliations, and accepts submissions from all interested parties. Submissions are reviewed by committee and advisors are referenced in the work's field.

Collaborators:
  • Dominic Burkart, founder (website)

To apply to have work included in Standard Deviation, email std.d3v@gmail.com with a subject line that reads "application" as well as your attached work and a short description or abstract.

  • We accept multiple applications, though ask that each application is sent independently as a separate email.
  • Standard formats (eg pdf, txt, jpg, tiff) are preferred. Large files (eg video files) can be sent as a link to a hosting site (eg google drive, dropbox, youtube, vimeo etc.).
  • The decision to include a work in Standard Deviation generally takes a few weeks.
  • We can't wait to see your work!
